Another blog?

Yep, I'm throwing my hat in the ring, and sharing things I've learnt throughout my career in design.

But who am I and what's my authority?

Well, to the second part "None, I have no authority".

What I do have is a ton of experience from 20+ years working in research and design, and at a wide variety of companies, big and small.

I've designed apps for Domino's Pizza, various transportation companies, UK government agencies, and startups. I've built and managed research and design teams. Oh, and I've also formed my own software startups (with the scars to prove it).

Now, I lead research and design at Workflowy, a productivity tool that helps people collect, organise, and turn their ideas into reality.

So, you can expect me to write about the macro and micro of designing software, from product strategy and design processes, all the way down to nerding out about keyboard shortcuts.

I'll also share tools/techniques that I've created along the way, hoping that if I save one person five minutes then it'll have been worth it.

Anyway, until next time, onwards.
